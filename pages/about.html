About Me

UX Designer • Front-End Developer • Product-Focused Problem Solver

Hi, I’m Alanah, a Computer Science student at UNC Charlotte with a focus in Human–Computer Interaction. I’m passionate about designing and building digital experiences that are intuitive, accessible, and centered around real user needs.

My work sits at the intersection of UX design and front-end development. I enjoy translating ideas into functional interfaces, whether that means researching user behavior, designing wireframes, or implementing solutions through code. I’m especially interested in how thoughtful design decisions can simplify complex tasks and improve everyday user experiences.

Through my projects, I’ve worked on UX research, interaction design, and web development, developing a strong foundation in both design thinking and technical execution. I value clarity, usability, and creating experiences that feel seamless to the user.

I’m currently seeking opportunities where I can continue growing as a UX designer and front-end developer while contributing to meaningful, user-focused products.
