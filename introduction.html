Roller Skate E-Commerce UX Evaluation

Course ITIS 3140 - User Experience Methods
Role: UX Researcher
Timeline: August - December, 2025

Evaluated three roller-skate e-commerce platforms to assess usability, information clarity, and navigation efficiency throughout the purchase journey.

My Role As A UX Researcher

  • Conducted heuristic evaluations across three platforms.
  • Analyzed product detail clarity, sizing guidance, and navigation
  • Synthesized findings with a team of researchers to identify usability gaps

Key Outcomes

  • Identified major friction points in sizing guidance and product hierarchy
  • Proposed a preliminary redesign concept addressing checkout flow clarity

What I Learned

Overall, this project strengthened my ability to translate qualitative observations into actionable UX recommendations while collaborating in a research-driven team.
